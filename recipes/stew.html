Beef Stew

Description

This hearty beef stew is an economical and easy way to create a wonderful dish for the winter months

Ingredients

  • 1kg beef chuck
  • 3 large onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Beef stock
  • Spices
  • Leftover veggies

Steps

  1. Sear meat on a dutch oven
  2. Remove the meat and cook onions until golden brown
  3. Add beef stock and meat
  4. Simmer for the 3 hours
  5. Add veggies and cook for another 15 mins
