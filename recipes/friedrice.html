Egg Fried Rice

This easy egg fried rice can be cooked in minutes using available ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Day old jasmine rice
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • Seasoning
  • Green onion
  • Optional: veggies

Steps

  1. Add eggs onto a hot skillet for 1 min
  2. Add rice and stir
  3. Add Seasonings and veggies
  4. Add veggies
  5. Add green onion to garnish, stir and serve
